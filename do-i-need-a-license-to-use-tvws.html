Does a White Space Device (WSD) need a license to use TVWS?

  1. FAQ
  2. Does a White Space Device (WSD) need a license to use TVWS?

White Space Devices (WSD) are license-exempt and do not require a license.

For more details on licensing information, please contact your nearest ISED regional or district office for more information.

You can also find more information on the ISED white space website.
